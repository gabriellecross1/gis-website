I have been asked to create a website that shows off the features and functions of both Google and Bing API's. I have included three maps on this website each one with a different purpose and using different tools such as a Keyhole Markup Language (KML) map and a GeoJSON map.

The theme of this website is America and when I decided on this theme I had people who are keen on travelling in mind. This website will help those who are thinking about travelling around America with information all about America's most famous landmarks, states they may want to avoid due to their high crime rate and the location of one of America's most famous roads and tourist destination: Route 66.
