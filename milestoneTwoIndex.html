I have been asked to create a website that shows off the features and functions of both Leaflet and Open Layer API's. I have included two maps on this website each one with a different purpose and using different tools such as a Keyhole Markup Language (KML) map and a GeoJSON map.

Following on from my American themed map I have decided to showcase features that Wales has to offer. This will aid people who want to travel around Wales, supplying information all about Wales' most famous landmarks, different transporation options around these landmarks and routes of the most popular lighthouses in Wales.
